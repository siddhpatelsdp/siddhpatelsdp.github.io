Top Trading Prop Firms: E8 Markets and MyFundedFX

E8 Markets is a leading prop firm known for its flexible and attractive profit-sharing model. It offers traders the opportunity to scale their accounts up to $1,000,000 by achieving monthly profit targets. The firm's scaling options, up to 16% per month, make it a highly rewarding platform for successful traders. Additionally, E8 Markets allows for trading across a variety of instruments, including forex and commodities, while offering a maximum leverage of 1:50 depending on the instrument.

MyFundedFX, on the other hand, is another popular choice for traders looking to manage significant capital. It provides up to 92.75% profit share and a clear scaling plan that increases your account by 25% every three months if you hit your profit targets. The firm offers leverage up to 1:200, which allows for more flexibility in executing trades. With account sizes ranging from $5,000 to $300,000, MyFundedFX is ideal for traders looking to grow their capital steadily over time.

Features of E8 Markets and MyFundedFX

  • E8 Markets: Up to 100% profit share for successful traders.
  • MyFundedFX: Flexible evaluation process and standard profit target in evaluation stages.
  • Both firms offer low-risk environments to scale trading capital.
